Why I Love the Game

I've loved basketball basically my whole life. Growing up I'd watch games whenever I could and play pickup at any gym I could get into. It's one of those things that just stuck with me.

Being at BYU, getting to watch the Cougars in the Big 12 has been awesome. The Marriott Center on a big game night is a completely different level of energy. It's loud, it's packed, and there's nothing quite like it.

What the game has taught me:

  1. Leadership
    • How to earn trust before giving direction
    • When to step up and when to step back
  2. Teamwork
    • Trusting others to do their job
    • Communicating clearly under pressure
  3. Resilience
    • Bouncing back after a bad game or a tough loss
    • Staying consistent even when things are not going your way
